About Me

My Introduction

I'm a counseling psychology trainee , Devadharshini, and I practice under supervision. My therapy approach is inclusive and centered around an array of evidence-based techniques. I offer persistent support to my clients while creating a secure and judgment-free space for exploration and healing. I offer so with understanding, compassion, and respect. I'm committed to supporting others in achieving personal growth and mental health because I truly would like to see other people flourish in life.Having witnessed the transformative power of therapy in my own life, I am dedicated to making a positive impact on the lives of those I work with, encouraging resilience and empowering them to lead more fulfilling lives.It's an honor and a joy to witness my clients' growth and resilience as they navigate their paths towards healing and self-discovery.

Let's walk this path together !!
